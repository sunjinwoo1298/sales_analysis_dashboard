# 📊 Business Performance Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end Business Intelligence dashboard built using **Power BI** and **SQL** to analyze retail sales performance across multiple markets, customers, and time periods. The project focuses on transforming raw transactional data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI reporting.


![Dashboard Preview](./assets/dashboard.png)

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Organizations generate large volumes of transactional data every day. This project demonstrates how business data can be transformed into meaningful insights using modern analytics tools.

The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, evaluate market performance, identify high-value customers, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance using historical sales data.
- Monitor revenue and sales quantity across different markets.
- Identify top-performing customers and regions.
- Track revenue trends over time.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business users and decision-makers.
- Enable dynamic filtering for exploratory analysis.

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Delhi NCR is the highest revenue-generating market, contributing more than 50% of the total revenue.
- Mumbai ranks as the second strongest market with significant contribution to overall sales.
- A small group of customers contributes a disproportionately large share of revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention.
- Revenue distribution varies considerably across markets, indicating opportunities for regional growth strategies.
- Revenue trends fluctuate over time, suggesting seasonal demand patterns that can support forecasting and inventory planning.
